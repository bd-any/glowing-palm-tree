Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course? expand_more
You can enroll by clicking the "Enroll Now" button, filling out the contact form, or visiting our center in person. Our team will guide you through the registration process.
Do you offer online classes? expand_more
Yes, we offer both offline (in-person) and online formats for most of our IT and language courses to suit your schedule.
What is the duration of the courses? expand_more
Course duration varies. Language courses typically run for 3-6 months per level, while IT bootcamps can range from 4 to 8 months depending on the intensity.
Is there a certificate upon completion? expand_more
Yes, upon successfully completing the course and passing the final exam, you will receive an official YuksalishLC certificate.
Do you help with job placement? expand_more
Absolutely. We have a dedicated career support team that helps top graduates with resume writing, interview preparation, and job referrals.
Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied? expand_more
We offer a trial period for the first week. If you decide not to continue during this period, you are eligible for a refund according to our policy.
