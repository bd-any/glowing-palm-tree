Enrollment FAQ

How do I pay for the courses?

expand_more
We accept payments via Click, Payme, bank transfer, and cash. You can pay monthly or for the full course duration with a discount.

Is there a refund policy?

expand_more
Yes, if you decide to stop within the first 3 lessons, we offer a full refund. After that, refunds are calculated based on remaining lessons.

Can I take a trial lesson?

expand_more
Absolutely! The first lesson of any course is free. It's a great way to meet the teacher and see if the course is right for you.

Do you provide certificates?

expand_more
Upon successful completion of the course and final exam, you will receive an official certificate from YuksalishLC.
